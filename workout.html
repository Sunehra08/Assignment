EXERCISES:

    CardioVascular Exercises:

  • Walking:Low impact, easy to encorporate into daily routines.
  • Running or jogging:Great for improving health and endurance.
  • Cycling:Good for leg strength and cardiovascular fitness.
  • Swimmimng:Full body workout that is gentle on the joints.
  • Jump rope:Excellent for coordination and cardiovascular endurance.

    Strength Training:

  • Body weight Exercises:Push ups squats , and lunges build strength without equipment
  • Weight Lifting:Using free weights or machines to target specific muscle group.
  • Resistance Bands:versatile and good for strength training at home.
  • Core Exercises:Planks , russians twists , and leg raises strengthn the core.

    Flexibility and Balance:

  • Yoga:improve Flexibility, balance and mental well being.
  • Pilates:focuses on core strength, flexibility and poster.
  • Streching: Daily streching can improve overall flexibility and prevent injuries
