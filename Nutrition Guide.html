NUTRITION GUIDE:

1. Balance Macronutrients:

  • Carbohydrates: Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables for energy
  • Proteins: Include lean meats, fish, eggs, legumes, and dairy for muscle repair.
  • Fats: Opt for healthy fats like avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil.

2.Include a Variety of Foods:

  • Aim for a colorful plate with diverse fruits and vegetables to ensure a range of nutrients.
    • Plan Ahead:
  • Dedicate time each week to plan meals and snacks, creating a shopping list to minimize impulse buys.

3. Prepare in Batches:

  • Cook larger portions of meals and store leftovers for quick access during busy days.

4.Healthy Snacks:

  • Prepare nutritious snacks (e.g., nuts, yogurt, fruit) to avoid unhealthy choices.

5.Stay Hydrated:

  • Include plenty of water throughout the day; consider herbal teas or infused water for variety.

6. Mind Portion Sizes:

  • Use smaller plates or bowls to help manage portion control and prevent overeating.

7. Limit Processed Foods:

  • Focus on whole, minimally processed foods while reducing added sugars and unhealthy fats.

8.Listen to Your Body:

  • Pay attention to hunger cues and eat mindfully, avoiding distractions during meals.

9. Experiment with Recipes:

  • Try new healthy recipes to keep meals interesting and enjoyable.

10.Track Your Intake:,

  • Consider using an app or journal to monitor what you eat, helping to stay accountable to your goals.
    • Incorporate Meal Timing:
  • Consider meal timing that works for your routine, such as regular meals and pre- or post-workout snacks.
    • Adjust as Needed:
  • Be flexible and adjust your meal plan based on your progress, cravings, and changes in activity levels.
